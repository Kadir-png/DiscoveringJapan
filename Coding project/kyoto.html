Overview

Kyoto, a Historic yet sleek city.

History

Fun Fact: Kyoto served as Japan's capital for over a thousand years and is home to countless cultural treasures!

Top Attractions

  • Fushimi Inari Shrine: known for its thousands of red torii gates, very hard to walk on!.
  • Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion): Kinkaku-ji is a temple covered in gold leaf.
  • Arashiyama Bamboo Grove: Arashiyama Bamboo Grove is a peaceful bamboo forest.

Regional Cuisine

Kyoto's cuisine is delicate and refined, focusing on seasonal ingredients. yudofu (tofu hot pot), Mochi (Japanese rice cake), and matcha (green tea) are amazing and a must-try!

Cultural Customs

Torists could try cultural ceremonies like Tea ceremonies and parades.
