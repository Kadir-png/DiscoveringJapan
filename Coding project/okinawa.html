Overview

Okinawa, a tropical paradise.

History

Okinawa was the capital of the independent Ryukyu Kingdom, which had a rich history and distinct culture

Top Attractions

  • Shurijo Castle: Shurijo Castle, a UNESCO World Heritage site, served as the Ryukyu Kingdom's royal palace!
  • Okinawa Churaumi Aquarium: One of the largest aquariums in the world!
  • Kokusai Street: The main shopping street filled with shops, restaurants, and cultural experiences.

Regional Cuisine

Local favorites include the stir-fried bitter melon goya champuru, Okinawa soba, and sweet deep-fried dough sata andagi.

Cultural Customs

Visitors can experience traditional music and dance during festivals.
