Overview

Osaka, vibrant nightlife and delicious street food, a must-visit!

History

Osaka has always been at the core of Japan's economic development. Today, Osaka is a bustling metropolis serving as Japan's major trade center.

Top Attractions

  • Osaka Castle: The famous historic castle located in Osaka city.
  • Dotonbori: The bright lights entertainment! It also has amazing food stalls/li>

Regional Cuisine

Be sure to try takoyaki (octopus balls), okonomiyaki (savory pancakes), and kushikatsu (deep-fried skewers).

Cultural Customs

When visiting Osaka respect local customs like queuing patiently and being polite in public spaces.
