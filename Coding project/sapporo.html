Overview

Sapporo, the largest city on the northern island of Hokkaido, is known for its stunning natural landscapes, winter sports, and delicious food.

History

Sapporo's history is relatively recent, with its development beginning in the late 19th century during the Meiji era.

Top Attractions

  • Sapporo Snow Festival: A famous event featuring massive snow sculptures.

Regional Cuisine

Sapporo is famous for its miso ramen and fresh seafood

Cultural Customs

Guests must understand the significance of bowing as a symbol of respect and the custom of taking off shoes before entering their homes.
